    How does our Express Cuisine's food delivery app works?'

    Browse

    Browse through hundreds of restaurants to choose from in our App. When you find something you like to order from a certain restaurant, tap to add it to your cart.

    Order

    When you’re ready to check out, you’ll see your address, an estimated delivery time, and the price of the order including tax and delivery fee. After confirming your order, we'll process your order.

    Track

    Our App has real-time tracking for you to keep updated. First you’ll see the restaurant accept and start prepping. Then, when the order’s almost ready, our Express Deliverer will go to the restaurant to pick it up and proceed to deliver the food to your location.

    • You no longer need to go outside to pick up the food yourself
    • As our business flourished, We also provide more job as a Express deliverer to deliver food.
    • Encourage people to support local products/restaurants

    This business is inspired by the food delivery service available in the Northern region of the world "Uber Eats". We are aiming expand our business throughout the country.
